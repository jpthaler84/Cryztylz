Cryztylz Collector

Greetings, traveler!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four magic crystals below. Choosing a crystal will add a specific amount of points to your score.

If you match your score exactly to mine, you win. If your score goes above, you lose!

The value of each crystal is hidden from your mortal eyes until you choose that crystal.

Each time you play, the value of each crystal changes. Good luck!
